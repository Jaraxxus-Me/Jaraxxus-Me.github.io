About me

I am pursuing B.ENG in Mechanical Engineering from Tongji University (TJU) as a senior undergraduate with top-ranking GPA 4.86/5.0 (2/130), National Scholarship, and Shanghai Scholarship, where I work in Vision4robotics Group.

I've been a research intern (RISS 2021), and will be an incoming Ph.D. student in the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University.

I'm now working with Prof. Hang Zhao in the MARS Lab at QiZhi Institute.

My research interest lies in Robot Perception in Constrained Environments, which includes: 1) Illumination Constrained, 2) Data Constrained, and 3) Computation Constarined.
