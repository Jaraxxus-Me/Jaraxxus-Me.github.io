About me

I am pursuing my bachelor degree in mechanical engineering from Tongji University (TJU) as a senior undergraduate with top-ranking GPA 4.86/5.0 (2/130), National Scholarship, and Shanghai Scholarship, where I work in Vision4robotics Group. Currently, I'm interning in the Airlab of Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, as a Robotics Institute Summer Scholar (RISS 2021).

My research interest lies in the visual perception for robots, whose goal is to design visual perception algorithms for robots' specific tasks. My research directions include: 1) Dark environment visual object tracking for unmanned aerial vehicles, 2) Directly applied few-shot detection for autonomous exploration, 3) Transformers for high-speed aerial tracking.
